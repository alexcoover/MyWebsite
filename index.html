About Me

Seattle-Based Composer, Sound Designer, and Software Developer

After earning my degree in Audio Engineering from Clackamas College, I began working as a freelance sound designer. I have contributed to dozens of film projects here in Seattle, ranging from foley and music composition, to mixing for theater. As my career progressed I grew interested in the technical side of audio, began to explore programming, and became immersed. I am now completing a bachelors degree in computer science and developing projects in a variety styles and languages in my free time, which you can see here. Check out the pages above to see/hear my work. For inquiries, please contact me via email, or any of the social media linked above.
