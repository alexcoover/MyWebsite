Midi Controller Project

A Completely Customized Instrument

Unable to find a small MIDI controller with pitch and modulation wheels, the solution was to build one from scratch. The project began with constructing, painting and wiring the box, and ended with flashing and adding some C++ to the arduino. Along the way I created a build guide which garnered enough attention to attract Digital Trends, who requested an interview.
